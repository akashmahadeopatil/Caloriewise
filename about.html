About Us

Track what you eat. Transform how you live.

🎯 Our Mission

CalorieWise was created with a simple mission: make nutrition tracking effortless, free, and accessible to everyone on the planet.

We believe that understanding what you eat is the first step toward a healthier life. But most calorie tracking apps are either too expensive, too complicated, or require you to create accounts and hand over your personal data. We wanted to change that.

🔥 What is CalorieWise?

CalorieWise is a free, smart calorie and nutrition tracker that works right in your browser. No downloads. No sign-ups. No subscriptions. Just open the website, type the food you ate, and get instant insights.

💡 Just type any food name → Get instant calorie count + full macro breakdown + personalized diet suggestions

👨‍💻 Who Built This?

CalorieWise was built by Akash Patil, a technology professional based in Pune, India, who is passionate about health, fitness, and making useful tools accessible to everyone.

This project started as a personal calorie tracker and grew into a full-fledged nutrition platform designed to help people worldwide make better dietary choices — without spending a single rupee.

🛠️ Technology

CalorieWise is built with modern web technologies for speed and simplicity:

  • HTML5 — Semantic, accessible structure
  • CSS3 — Custom properties, animations, responsive grid layout
  • Vanilla JavaScript — Zero frameworks, zero dependencies, lightning fast
  • USDA FoodData Central API — World's most comprehensive food nutrition database

The entire application runs from a single HTML file — no servers, no databases, no complex infrastructure. This means it loads instantly and works even on slow connections.
