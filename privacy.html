Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

📋 Overview

At CalorieWise, we take your privacy extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website caloriewise.netlify.app ("the Website").

🔒 TL;DR: We collect almost nothing. Your food data stays in YOUR browser. We don't have accounts, we don't track you, and we don't sell any data.

1️⃣ Information We Collect

Short answer: Almost nothing.

CalorieWise is designed to be a privacy-first application. Here's what we do and don't collect:

  • Food log data: All food items you log are stored only in your browser's memory (RAM). They are NOT sent to any server. When you close the tab, all data is gone.
  • USDA API key: If you choose to connect the USDA API, your API key is stored in your browser's localStorage — on YOUR device only. We never see it or store it on any server.
  • Calorie goal: Your daily calorie goal setting is stored locally in your browser only.
  • No personal information: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any personal details through the CalorieWise tracker.
  • No accounts: There is no user registration, login, or account system. You remain completely anonymous.

2️⃣ Cookies

The CalorieWise tracker itself does not use cookies. However:

  • localStorage: We use browser localStorage to save your USDA API key (if provided). You can clear this anytime via your browser settings.
  • Third-party cookies: If we display advertisements (e.g., Google AdSense), those ad networks may use cookies to show relevant ads. See Section 5 for details.

3️⃣ Third-Party Services

CalorieWise may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • USDA FoodData Central API: When you search for food with the API connected, your search query is sent to the USDA's servers (api.nal.usda.gov) to retrieve nutritional data. This is governed by the USDA's own privacy policy.
  • Google AdSense: We may display advertisements provided by Google. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out at Google Ad Settings.
  • Google Fonts: We load the Inter font from Google Fonts. This involves a request to Google's servers.
  • Netlify: Our website is hosted on Netlify, which may collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type, access time) as part of standard web hosting. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.

4️⃣ How We Use Information

Any information processed by CalorieWise is used solely to provide the calorie tracking functionality — calculating your nutrition totals and generating diet suggestions. We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share your data with third parties (except as described in Section 3)
  • Use your data for profiling or marketing
  • Store your data on any server we control

5️⃣ Advertising

We may display third-party advertisements on our website to support the free service. These advertising partners (such as Google AdSense) may use technologies like cookies and web beacons to collect information about your browsing activities across websites in order to provide targeted advertising.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ad Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out

6️⃣ Data Security

Since we don't collect or store personal data on any server, the security risk is minimal. Your food log exists only in your browser's temporary memory. The USDA API key in localStorage is accessible only from your device.

Our website is served over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption), ensuring all data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted.

7️⃣ Children's Privacy

CalorieWise is a general-purpose nutrition tool. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, this risk is inherently minimal.

8️⃣ Your Rights

Since we don't store your personal data, there is nothing to delete or export. However, you can:

  • Clear localStorage: Go to your browser settings → Clear site data for our domain
  • Disable cookies: Adjust your browser settings to block third-party cookies
  • Opt out of ads: Use the links provided in Section 5

9️⃣ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

🔟 Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
