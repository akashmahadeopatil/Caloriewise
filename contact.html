💡 Frequently Asked Questions

Is CalorieWise really free?

Yes, 100% free! No hidden charges, no premium plans, no subscriptions. CalorieWise is and will always be free to use.

Do I need to create an account?

No. CalorieWise requires zero sign-up. Just open the website and start tracking. Your privacy is our priority.

How do I add foods not in the database?

Connect the free USDA FoodData Central API for access to 300,000+ foods worldwide. Click the "Connect USDA API" button on the home page to get started.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All your food data stays in your browser only — nothing is sent to any server. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use CalorieWise on my phone?

Yes! CalorieWise is fully responsive and works beautifully on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
